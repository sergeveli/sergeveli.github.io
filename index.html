Repo

Marvelous Movie Reviews

Repo

Marvelous is an app based on GoodReads that allows users to review Marvel movies and add movies to user's profile bookshelf. Marvelous was created with Sequelize, JavaScript, Express and PUG.

Shamstagram

Repo

A website where users can post fake images about their fake life to their fake friends. This was built on React-Redux on the front end and Flask, SQL-Alchemy and AWS S3 on the backend.

Pley

Repo

A YELP clone focused on providing a directory of local indoor basketball gyms with ratings and reviews. Pley was built on a frontend consisting of React-Redux and backend consisting of Express and PostgreSQL.
